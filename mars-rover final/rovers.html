NASA Mars Exploration Program

Rovers on
the Red Planet

Two robotic explorers sent millions of miles to study Mars and search for signs of past life.

Meet the Rovers

Curiosity

Launched 2011 · MSL Mission

Curiosity studies Mars’ climate and geology to determine whether it ever supported microbial life.

Perseverance

Launched 2020 · Mars 2020 Mission

Perseverance searches for ancient life and collects rock samples for future return to Earth.
